Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and card payments. Enterprise customers can arrange invoice-based billing.

Is the offline version a separate product?

The offline-only version is a standalone application with a one-time licence fee. It shares the same core features as the cloud platform but runs entirely on local devices without requiring internet.

Can I mix cloud and offline deployments?

Absolutely. Many organisations run cloud at their headquarters and offline at remote collection points. Data syncs automatically when connectivity is available.

Do you offer discounts for cooperatives?

Yes. We offer special pricing for farmer cooperatives and community-based organisations. Contact our sales team for details.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains available for 90 days after cancellation. You can export everything to CSV at any time. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted.
