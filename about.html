About KilimoTech

Purpose-built for how agriculture actually works

Designed from the ground up for the realities of agricultural management in East Africa and beyond — unreliable internet, diverse crops, complex supply chains, and operations that span from rural collection points to export warehouses.

Our Mission

Digitise agricultural operations across Africa

Most agricultural operations in Africa still run on paper ledgers, manual weighing records, and spreadsheets passed around on USB drives. This leads to data loss, payment disputes, traceability gaps, and inefficiency.

KilimoTech replaces all of that with a single platform that works where farming happens — at the collection point, the factory floor, the drying yard, and the store room. Online or offline.

Built by the KilimoTech team, focused on building software for agricultural and industrial operations in Africa.

What we believe

🌿

Built for the field

We design for the realities of agricultural operations — unreliable internet, dusty environments, busy collection points, and early mornings.

📡

Offline first

Internet is a luxury in many farming regions. Every feature works offline by default, with sync as a bonus rather than a requirement.

⚙

Configurable, not rigid

No two operations are identical. Workflows, modules, and processes are configurable per tenant — because your operation knows best.

🔒

Data belongs to you

Whether in our cloud, on your servers, or on a device in your pocket — your data is always yours to export, migrate, or delete.

✨

Simple by design

Factory supervisors and store clerks shouldn't need training manuals. Clean interfaces, clear actions, and sensible defaults.

🌍

African context

Built with East African agriculture in mind — M-Pesa integration, KRA compliance readiness, Swahili-ready, and local support.
