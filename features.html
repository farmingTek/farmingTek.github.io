Platform Features

Everything your operation needs

Purpose-built modules for agricultural operations — from intake at the gate to export-ready inventory. Configure workflows to match your exact process.

⚖

Cherry Intake & Weighing

Identify farmers via barcode, QR, RFID, or manual entry. Capture weight from connected scales in real time with configurable inspection workflows. Supports multiple identification methods per tenant.

  • Barcode, QR, RFID, or manual farmer identification
  • Live weight capture from RS-232 / USB scales
  • Configurable inspection step before weighing
  • Automatic receipt printing (thermal, A4, or SMS)
  • Cumulative season totals per farmer
☘

Farmer & Picker Registry

Maintain records for thousands of farmers and pickers. Track season totals, delivery history, and print statements — all searchable and filterable.

  • Unlimited farmer and picker records
  • Season-by-season delivery tracking
  • CSV import and export
  • ID card generation with barcode/QR
  • Farmer statement printing
◆

Grading & Batching

Grade produce post-fermentation, track weight loss percentages, and batch items for downstream processing. Full traceability from farmer to final grade.

  • Grade classification (AA, AB, C, etc.)
  • Pre- and post-fermentation weight tracking
  • Automatic weight loss percentage calculation
  • Batch creation and management
  • Full farmer-to-grade traceability
☀

Drying Operations

Monitor drying beds in real time. Track days on bed, record dry weights, and get alerts when batches are ready for the next stage.

  • Visual drying bed status board
  • Day count tracking per batch
  • Dry weight recording
  • Ready-for-collection alerts
  • Historical drying data and averages
▦

Inventory & Stores

Manage fertilizer and chemical stores with bin cards, stock alerts, expiry tracking, and authorisation thresholds for dispensing.

  • Separate fertilizer and chemical stores
  • Bin card system with full movement history
  • Low stock and expiry alerts on dashboard
  • Authorisation thresholds for high-value dispensing
  • Receipt and dispatch workflows
▤

Reports & Analytics

Generate operational reports from farmer delivery statements to yield analysis. Export to PDF or CSV for regulatory and management needs.

  • Farmer delivery statements
  • Daily and weekly intake summaries
  • Batch traceability reports
  • Yield and conversion analysis
  • Fertilizer and chemical stock reports
  • Full audit trail export
👤

User Management & RBAC

Role-based access control with predefined roles — Administrator, Manager, Factory Supervisor, Store Clerk, and Viewer. Each role sees only the modules they need.

  • 5 predefined roles with granular permissions
  • Per-module access control (full, read, own, none)
  • User activity tracking
  • Password reset and account management
  • Audit log of all user actions
⚙

Workflow Configuration

Every workflow is configurable per tenant. Enable or disable inspection, choose identification methods, configure receipt formats — all from a settings panel.

  • Enable or disable workflow steps per tenant
  • Choose farmer identification method
  • Configure receipt format and printing
  • Set scale connection protocol and parameters
  • Store authorisation thresholds
  • Module enable/disable toggles
