Privacy Policy

How KilimoTech collects, uses, and protects your personal and organisational data. Last updated February 2026.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, and organisation details provided during setup.
  • Operational data: farmer records, delivery data, weights, grades, and inventory records entered into the platform.
  • Usage data: how you interact with the platform, including pages visited, features used, and device information.
  • For offline deployments: data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you enable sync.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the KilimoTech platform.
  • To authenticate users and manage access control.
  • To send important service notifications (security alerts, maintenance windows).
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.
  • We never sell your personal or operational data to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

  • Cloud deployments: data is stored in secure, encrypted databases with regular backups.
  • On-premise deployments: data resides entirely on your infrastructure.
  • Offline deployments: data is stored locally on the device in an encrypted database.
  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
  • Access to production systems is restricted and audited.

Data Retention

  • Your operational data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • After account cancellation, data is available for export for 90 days, then permanently deleted.
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 30 additional days in encrypted form.
  • For offline deployments, data retention is managed entirely by you on your local device.

Your Rights

  • You can export all your data at any time in CSV format.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • You can update or correct your personal information at any time through the platform.

Cookies & Tracking

  • We use essential cookies for authentication and session management.
  • We use anonymous analytics to understand platform usage patterns.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers.
  • You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

Contact

  • For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at privacy@kilimotech.africa.
  • We will respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
